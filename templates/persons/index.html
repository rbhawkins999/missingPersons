Human Trafficking

What is human trafficking? According to the Utah Office of the Attorney General website, human trafficking is “a modern-day form of slavery involving the illegal trade of people for exploitation or commercial gain.” It can involve all types of people from all walks of life, not just the stereotypical poor individual born in another country. This is a hidden crime where victims may struggle to seek help because of fear or threat of violence. This is an estimated $32 billion dollar per year industry. Is this the kind of booming industry we want in our communities?
